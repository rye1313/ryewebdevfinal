Ryleigh MacDonald is a queer contemporary artist who splits her time between Portland & Chicago, studying Photography at Columbia College Chicago. With a passion for Alternative Process and Experimental Photography, her Sophomore year in college has been well-spent working with Cyanotype, VanDyke Brown, and Lumen sun-exposure photo methods. Rye explores 19th century photography methods through a modern lens, a focus on all things strange, dark and beautiful.
